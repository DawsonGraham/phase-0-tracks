This page is dedicated to the sport of baseball. Baseball is America's past time and one of the greatest traditions/sports of our culture. The main organization is called the MLB (Major League Baseball) and there are two leagues that the teams compete in:

  1. The American League
  2. The National League
Within these leagues, there are 6 separate divisions that teams fall under, which include:
  • AL East
  • AL Central
  • AL West
  • NL East
  • NL Central
  • NL West
To find out more information, please visit the official site of the MLB.
